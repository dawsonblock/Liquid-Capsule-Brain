1. Get the service URL:
  If you enabled ingress (default):
    https://{{ (index .Values.ingress.hosts 0).host }}
  Otherwise, port-forward:
    kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "capsule-brain.fullname" . }} 8000:80

2. Health:
    curl -k https://{{ (index .Values.ingress.hosts 0).host }}/healthz

3. Metrics (Prometheus):
    Ensure ServiceMonitor CRD/operator installed (values.metrics.serviceMonitor.create=true).
